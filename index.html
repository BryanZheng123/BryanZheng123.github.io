Fortnite is an online video game developed and made by the company of Epic Games and was released in 2017. It is available in three entirely different game mode versions that otherwise share the same general gameplay and game engine: Fortnite Battle Royale, a free-to-play battle royale game in which can take up to 100 players (not bots) to fight to be the last person standing; Fortnite: Save the World, a cooperative hybrid tower defense-shooter and survival game in which up to four players, that could be your friends, your clan, or just other random people around the world to fight off zombie-like creatures and defend objects with traps and fortifications they can build; and Fortnite: Creative, in which players are given infinite amount of resources to build and has complete freedom to create worlds and battle arenas.
